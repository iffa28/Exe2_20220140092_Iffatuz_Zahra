About Stray Kids

Stray Kids, formed by JYP Entertainment in 2017, is a South Korean boy band that has quickly risen to global fame. The group is known for its high-energy performances, deeply personal lyrics, and the unique ability of its members to compose and produce their own music. The band consists of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N.

Their music covers a range of genres and often addresses themes of mental health, self-acceptance, and the pressures faced by the youth of today, making them stand out in the K-pop industry. With a dedicated fan base known as 'STAY,' Stray Kids continues to influence and inspire a global audience.

Stray Kids has been acknowledged with numerous awards for their musical versatility and powerful stage presence. They continue to push the boundaries of K-pop, exploring new sounds and concepts, proving themselves as one of the most influential and dynamic groups in the modern music scene.
